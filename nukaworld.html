NUKA-WORLD

After 11 years of Nuka-Cola's success on the national market, the soda-themed amusement park, Nuka-World, opened its gates for the first time on May 1, 2050.On opening day, over 40,000 tickets were sold. Due to the overwhelming success, plans to expand beyond the original two parks, Nuka-Town USA and Kiddie Kingdom, were set in motion immediately. The additions included Dry Rock Gulch in 2058, followed by Safari Adventure in 2067. The result was ticket sales nearly doubling overnight.
